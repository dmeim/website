# Product Vision

## Purpose

Create a fast, modern personal website for dmeim.com that serves as:

- Identity hub with contact info and social links (expanding later)
- Resume (dedicated `/resume` page)
- Portfolio (future)
- Public key distribution and verification hub

## Principles

- Static-first: simple deploys, maximal speed and reliability
- Minimal JavaScript: only where interaction is needed
- Accessible and inclusive: semantic HTML, keyboard-friendly, good contrast
- Maintainable: clear asset folders; content easy to update

## Audience

- Recruiters and collaborators (resume, contact)
- Technical peers (keys, verification instructions)
- General visitors (overview and links)
